FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the AI calorie counter.

Is CalTracker free?
Yes. CalTracker is free to download and includes a free forever plan with full calorie and macro tracking, water and weight tracking, and 20 lifetime AI food scans. Optional Plus and Pro plans unlock more daily AI scans and advanced AI models.
How does the AI food scanner work?
Just snap a photo of your meal. CalTracker's AI recognises the food and instantly estimates the calories, protein, carbs and fat, then logs them to your daily diary — so you don't have to search databases or weigh everything by hand.
How accurate is an AI calorie counter?
AI calorie counters give fast, close estimates that are ideal for tracking trends and staying in a calorie deficit or surplus. CalTracker lets you review and fine-tune every entry, so your log gets more accurate the more you use it.
What can I track with CalTracker?
Calories, protein, carbs and fat (macros), body weight, water intake, steps and activity, daily streaks, and a weekly overview of your progress. An AI daily evaluation even scores your day and suggests how to improve.
Is CalTracker available for iPhone and Android?
Yes. CalTracker is available on both the App Store for iPhone and Google Play for Android, with cloud sync so your data stays up to date across devices.
