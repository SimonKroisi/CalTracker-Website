Delete Your Account

Permanently delete your CalTracker account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data — including meals, weight entries, water logs, streaks, AI insights, favorites, and preferences — will be permanently removed. If you only want to delete specific data, visit the Data Deletion page instead.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open CalTracker

Launch the app and sign in to the account you want to delete.

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon in the navigation bar.

3

Navigate to Account → Delete Account

Find the Account section and tap "Delete Account".

4

Confirm Deletion

Read the warning carefully and confirm that you want to permanently delete your account and all data.

Prefer email?

Send us an email from the address associated with your account and we'll process the deletion for you.

caltracker.track@gmail.com

What Data Will Be Deleted

Account & Profile

Your login credentials, display name, profile picture, and all preferences.

All Meal Entries

Every logged meal, snack, calorie data, and macro breakdown.

Weight & Health Data

Weight entries, trend data, and synced health platform data.

Water Logs

All water intake records and hydration history.

Streaks & Progress

Your current streak, longest streak, and all progress history.

Favorites & AI Insights

Saved favorites, AI-generated insights, and nutritional recommendations.

Retention Timeline

30 Days Account data is permanently deleted from primary servers.
90 Days Data is removed from all backup systems.
14 Months Anonymized analytics data may be retained (cannot be linked to you).
