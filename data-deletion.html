Data Deletion

Want to remove specific data while keeping your account? Here's how to delete individual data types from CalTracker.

Types of Data You Can Delete

Meal Entries

Individual meals, snacks, and all associated calorie & macro data.

Weight Entries

Weight logs, trend data, and body measurement records.

Water Logs

Daily water intake records and hydration history.

AI Insights

AI-generated nutritional insights and personalized recommendations.

Favorites

Saved favorite meals and frequently used food items.

Profile Data

Display name, profile picture, and personal preferences.

How to Delete Your Data

1

Open CalTracker

Launch the CalTracker app on your device and make sure you're signed in.

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon in the navigation bar to access app settings.

3

Navigate to Account

Find the Account section in your settings.

4

Select Data to Delete

Choose which specific data types you want to remove. You can delete individual entries or categories.

5

Confirm Deletion

Review your selection and confirm. The selected data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Prefer email?

You can also request data deletion by emailing us. Include your account email and specify which data you'd like removed.

caltracker.app@gmail.com

Processing Timeline

Immediate: Data is removed from the app and no longer visible to you.

Within 30 days: Data is permanently deleted from our primary servers.

Within 90 days: Data is removed from all backup systems.

Anonymized analytics: May be retained for up to 14 months and cannot be linked back to you.
