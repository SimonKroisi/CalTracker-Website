Data Deletion

Want to remove specific data while keeping your account? Here's how to delete individual data types from CalTracker.

Types of Data You Can Delete

🍽️

Meal Entries

Delete individual meals, snacks, or clear entire days of food logs and calorie data.

⚖️

Weight Entries

Remove individual weight records or clear your entire weight history and trend data.

💧

Water Logs

Delete water intake entries for specific days or clear all hydration data.

🤖

AI Insights

Remove AI-generated nutritional insights and analysis history.

⭐

Favorites

Clear your saved favorite meals, foods, and quick-add items.

👤

Profile Data

Update or remove your profile picture, display name, and personal preferences.

How to Delete Your Data

1

Open CalTracker

Launch the app and sign in to your account.

2

Navigate to the Data

Go to the section containing the data you want to delete (e.g., Food Log, Weight, Water).

3

Select & Delete

Tap or swipe on the individual entry you want to remove, then confirm the deletion.

4

Bulk Delete (Optional)

For clearing all data of a type, go to Settings → Account → Manage Data and select what to clear.

5

Confirm

Review what will be deleted and confirm. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

Processing Timeline

Immediate Data is removed from the app and no longer visible to you.
30 Days Data is permanently deleted from primary servers.
90 Days Data is removed from all backup systems.
14 Months Anonymized analytics may be retained (cannot be linked to you).
