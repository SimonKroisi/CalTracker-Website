Weight & Body Metrics

How it works

Designed to be simple

Just snap a pic

Point your camera at any meal. CalTracker's AI detects individual food items, estimates portions, and calculates calories and macros — all in seconds.

  • Detects multiple items in one photo
  • Shows confidence percentage per item
  • Full macro breakdown (P / C / F)
  • Select or deselect items before logging

Track your progress

See today's activity at a glance — calories burned from steps and workouts, step count with goals, and your weight trend over time.

  • Calories burned from steps + workouts
  • Step counter with daily goal
  • Weight chart with trend line
  • Health Connect & Apple Health sync

Let AI rate your meals

Get a personalized daily nutrition score from 0 to 100. See your energy balance, individual meal scores, and detailed category breakdowns.

  • Overall score with AI explanation
  • Energy balance (consumed − burned = net)
  • Individual meal scores
  • Macros, Timing & Quality breakdown

Compare your meals

Browse your full history with daily summaries. See averages, days on target, total meals, and compare your macro intake day by day.

  • Average calories per day
  • Days on target counter
  • Daily AI score per entry
  • Color-coded calorie progress
